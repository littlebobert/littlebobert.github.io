Sasu

A macOS app for asking about the page, form, or app on your screen.
Download for macOS
Requires macOS 13 or newer, an OpenAI API key, and Screen Recording permission.

What it does:

  • Press a global hotkey to prepare a screenshot.
  • Ask a question like “Explain this” or “Where do I click?”
  • Get a concise answer, plus optional on-screen guidance.

Why I built it:

I live in Japan and sometimes need help understanding forms, settings pages, and unfamiliar UI. Sasu makes the screenshot-and-ask workflow fast enough to use in the moment.

Privacy:

Sasu has no backend. Your OpenAI API key is stored locally in macOS Keychain, and screenshots are sent directly from your Mac to OpenAI only when you ask a question.
